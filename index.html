Hello, I am Dessiree Henry

I’m a Graphic & UX/UI designer. I specialize in creating intuitive and high quality experiences that are geared towards achieving the marketing goals of my clients. My focus is on  finding creative solutions to users & clients problems. Combining the perfect balance of efficiency, aesthetic, and functionality. By thoughtfully selecting color schemes, typography and interactive elements i aim to create designs that are not only visually appealing but user-focused.  My approach to creating digital experiences  is prioritizing understanding the users’ needs, behaviours, and expectations. This deep understanding allows me to design experiences that genuinely resonate with the user, resulting in impactful and meaningful designs.

My UX Skills

USER RESEARCH

My approach is to focus on the users needs, behaviours and preferences. To gain insights that will inform the design, development and improvement of the clients products, services or experience. Utilizing interviews,surveys,Usability testing,card sorting,user personas, user journey map

USER EMPATHY

My approach is with a genuine curiosity listening, observing and engaging with users to develop a deep understanding of their perspectives, challenges and desires. Utilizing User interviews and Surveys

QUALITATIVE INTERVIEWS

My approach is to be flexible and conversational. I encourage participants to provide detailed responses and express their viewpoints. Utilizing user interviews and user testing.
